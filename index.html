About the Broker

A Strategic Perspective on Butuan's Growth.

Real estate in Butuan City is no longer just about finding a house; it is about identifying an asset that grows with the city's evolving landscape.

I am Stef Co, a PRC-licensed real estate professional dedicated to providing a refined, data-driven approach to property acquisition. With a deep understanding of Agusan del Norte's urban expansion from the burgeoning commercial hubs in Libertad to the serene residential enclaves of Villa Kananga, my goal is to bridge the gap between high-potential inventory and discerning investors.

My practice is built on three non-negotiable pillars:

  • Rigorous Due Diligence: I personally vet every listing, ensuring titles are clean, developers are reputable, and structural integrity meets a professional standard.

  • Seamless Execution: From the first site viewing to the final transfer of title, I handle the complexities of documentation and bank financing so you can focus on your legacy.
